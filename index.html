    About Our Store
    We are a small young brand of leather goods. We create handmade bags, purses, and accessories from high-quality materials and with a great passion for our job. We create stylish things with original design solutions. We value each detail of every product and create items that will make you happy.
    About Our Delivery
    We deliver around the city in 1–2 days within the city premises, which costs €6. The delivery beyond the city costs $12. We can also arrange a postal delivery to other regions for $6. If the good you’re interested in is not in stock, it will take 1-7 days to produce it before sending it to the customer.
